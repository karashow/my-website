Hi, I am Eugenio from Italy 🇮🇹 Since October 2022, I am pursuing my PhD at <a href="https://cispa.de/en" target="_blank">CISPA Helmholtz Center for Information Security</a>, where I work in the awsome group of <a href="https://lucjanhanzlik.github.io/" target="_blank">Lucjan Hanzlik</a>. Before that I was doing my studies in <a href="https://www.dm.unipi.it/en/" target="_blank"> Matemathics</a> at <a href="https://www.unipi.it/en/" target="_blank"> Università di Pisa</a>, where I graduated *cum laude* under the supervision of <a href="https://people.dm.unipi.it/lombardo/" target="_blank"> Davide Lombardo</a>.

My research focuses on **Cryptography**, in particular on the design and analysis of <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Post-quantum_cryptography" target="_blank"> post-quantum </a> cryptosystems with provable security guarantees. Given my background in math, I am also interested in the more mathematical aspects of cryptography, especially lattices and isogenies. 

I am passionated about advanced <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Digital_signature" target="_blank">signature schemes</a> such as <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Blind_signature" target="_blank"> blind signatures</a>. I enjoy both designing new schemes and studying the security of existing ones. More recently, I have started looking into <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Blind_signature" target="_blank">oblivious PRFs</a>, a direction I would like to explore further. You can find an up to date list of my publications [here]({{ '/pubblications/' | relative_url }}).

Outside of academic research, I enjoy solving cryptographic challenges in Capture-The-Flag (CTF) competitions, often participating with the <a href="https://x.com/aboutblankets" target="_blank">about:blankets</a> team from Torino. See also the [Misc]({{ '/misc/' | relative_url }}) page.  
I am also a big fan of good coffee, books, long walks around the city or in nature, and techno music.

If you have any questions, are interested in my research, or just want to talk about cryptography, feel free to <a href="mailto:eugenio.paracucchi@cispa.de"> reach out!</a>
